Work

We use the iTero scanner to take accurate digital impressions of your teeth. It captures snapshots of your teeth and gums at 6000 frames per second and gives us a 3D visualization of your teeth and bite. The iTero allows us to make super precise study models, aligners, and appliances, faster and without goopy impressions!
